Song Space needs a modern desktop browser

      
This experience uses your webcam, the Web Audio API, and WebAssembly to turn
         movement into music. Your browser is missing one of those.

      
Try the latest Chrome, Edge, or Safari on a laptop or desktop.

      

    

  


  
  

    

      
Best on a laptop

      
Song Space reads your whole body through the webcam, so it wants room and a
         bigger screen. Come back on a laptop or desktop with a camera for the full thing.
