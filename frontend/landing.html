Song Space

Your movement plays the music.

Step in front of your camera and dance. Song Space turns a song into a living soundscape that you shape in real time. How you move, where you go, how still you get, it all blends, layers, and transforms the music around you.

📷 You'll need a webcam. Song Space watches how you move to drive the music. Nothing works without a camera.
🔒 Your video stays with you. Everything is processed in your browser and never uploaded. No video ever leaves your device.
💻 Works best on a laptop with a webcam, with room to move and the sound turned up.
Start dancing

Give the browser camera permission when it asks, then pick a song.
